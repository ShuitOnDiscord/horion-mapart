Horion Mapart Converter

Convert mapart from MapartCraft to Horion NBT

Unfortunately, due to limitations with Minecraft NPCs, all setblock and fill commands cannot be put into one.
Due to this limitation, have to split the commands into multiple NPCs, placing them one after the other and pressing the Build button.

Tips for MapartCraft:
  • Disable staircasing for a completely flat schematic, only use if you NEED the extra color depth.

Tips for placing:
  • After the first NPC, break the cobblestone block above the NPC before placing the others.
  • If staircasing is off, then place the NPCs one block north of the top block of the map,
    this is to compensate for MapartCraft putting a cobblestone line at the top of the map.

  • Keep in mind that block placing follows the limits of setblock and fill commands,
    so if your art is multiple maps (more that 2x2) split it into 1x1 chunks so it doesn't fail to place

Usage:
  • Visit MapArtcraft and import your image
  • From the left, select the blocks you want to use, if you're not sure, just use the Everything preset
  • Adjust the options on the right (mess around with dithering and brightness to make your image look best)
  • Hit the Download NBT button, and import the downloaded .nbt file on this website
  • Wait a few seconds for the converter to do its magic, and either copy the nbt in the text box, or press the Download button.
